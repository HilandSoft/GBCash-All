As it is a short-term cash flow solution, we highly suggest you to pay off the loan as early as possible. However, if an emergency arises and you are unable to pay your installment on the due date, we recommend the extension option to avoid late charges and remain in good standing with Golden Bridge Cash SolutionTM for future short-term loan options.

You can request an extension by entering the members section, and simply choose the installment you want to extend from the original repayment schedule. Make sure you receive a confirmation that your extension was submitted successfully.

An extension request must be received no later than 2 business days before your payment due date. The extension fee is the finance fee of the current principle balance and is debited on the original due date.
